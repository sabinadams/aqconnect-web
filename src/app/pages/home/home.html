About AQConnect


This project is dedicated to helping unite and build the AQ3D Community! Meant to be used as a meeting place, info broker, social interaction tool, and more!

We started with a simple app, but as our community has grown and a greater need for maximum useablility of AQConnect arose, we decided it was time to get serious and build a website.

We hope you enjoy our growing community! If you have any suggestions, concerns, problems please feel free to contact us and we will be happy to talk with you.
